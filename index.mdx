## What is Builddrr?

Builddrr is an innovative AI website builder that revolutionizes how you create websites. Instead of wrestling with complex design tools or code, you simply **chat with our AI** to bring your vision to life.

### Key Features

- **🤖 AI-Powered Conversations**: Describe what you want in plain language
- **⚡ Instant Generation**: Watch your website come to life in real-time
- **🎨 Professional Design**: Beautiful, modern layouts automatically created
- **📱 Responsive by Default**: Works perfectly on all devices
- **🚀 No Coding Required**: Perfect for entrepreneurs, small businesses, and anyone
- **☁️ Hosting Included**: Free deployment and hosting are included in your plan
- **📦 Full Code Export**: Download your complete project to host anywhere you want
